# 🏆 World Cup 2026 — Bracket Tracker

A single-page, visual knockout bracket for the **2026 FIFA World Cup** (USA · Canada · Mexico).
Round of 32 → Final, with dates, kickoff times (ET) and venues for every match.

**Tap any match to light up the road it leads to the Final** — each step shows the date and
venue, so it doubles as a ticket-planning tool: you can see exactly where a team would play
on a deep run before tickets sell out.

- **Bracket view** — the full two-sided tournament tree with connector lines.
- **Schedule list** — every match by round, with local stadium times too.
- A "where are we now" stage stepper and a next-match banner update based on the current date.

Schedule per the official 2026 FIFA World Cup fixture list. Matchups in the bracket fill in
("Winner Group I", "3rd Group C/D/F", etc.) as FIFA confirms group results.

*Fan-made, just for fun — not affiliated with FIFA.*
